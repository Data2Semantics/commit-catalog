Adding your data or vocabulary to the catalog will take just two minutes and is as easy as answering 7 simple questions. Do so now, and take the survey!

The COMMIT/ data and vocabulary catalog is an initiative of three COMMIT/ projects running at VU Amsterdam, Data2Semantics, SEALINCMedia and METIS.

The catalog is intended to share expertise and spark synergy between different COMMIT/ projects. Data and vocabularies can be added by filling in a simple web form.
